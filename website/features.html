SubTrack
Features

Subscriptions, expenses, invoices, and reminders.

Subscription Tracking

Track active subscriptions, monthly totals, billing cycles, yearly run rate, and upcoming renewals.

Expense Reports

Record one-time expenses, filter by time period, and review category-based spending trends.

Invoice Management

Organize invoice records, monitor paid and unpaid status, scan receipts, and export PDFs.

AI Reminders

Create reminder drafts for renewals, bills, transactions, and personal tasks using natural language.
