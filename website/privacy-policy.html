Privacy Policy

SubTrack is designed to help you manage subscriptions, expenses, invoices, and reminders. This policy summarizes the types of data the app may use to provide those features.

Data You Provide

You may enter subscription details, expense records, invoice information, reminder notes, and receipt images. This data is used to provide app functionality.

Cloud Services

SubTrack may use Firebase services for authentication, data storage, analytics, and app functionality. AI parsing features may send submitted text or receipt content to a secure backend endpoint for processing.

Advertising and Analytics

The app may use advertising and analytics services to understand usage and support the app. Device identifiers may be used where permitted by platform settings.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact support@subtrack-manager.vercel.app.
